The Eidos Method

A four-phase, evidence-first approach that exposes critical dependencies, architects cohesive solutions, and transfers capability—so transformation sticks long after the slide deck.

1. Illuminate Dependencies

Map the hidden links between services, data, processes, and vendors—creating a single source of truth for risk and opportunity.

2. Analyze Systemic Dynamics

Quantify how those dependencies impact performance, cost, risk, and strategic agility—prioritizing the changes that move the needle.

3. Architect Cohesive Solutions

Design service flows, operating models, and governance that proactively manage dependencies and align execution to strategy.

4. Embed, Measure, Evolve

Implement iteratively, transfer know-how to your teams, and establish metrics that keep the ecosystem resilient and adaptable.
