Who I Am & How I Work

I’m Avery McKie, the integration architect behind Eidos. When a project needs deep, specialized expertise, I engage a vetted network of engineers, service-designers, and governance analysts—delivering big-firm capability without big-firm overhead.

Origin Story

Eidos took shape after I repeatedly watched transformation programs stall on unseen dependencies—scattered data, siloed accountability, governance that couldn’t keep pace. I codified a practical playbook to surface and manage those hidden links. That framework became the Eidos Method.

Today I apply that method to help telecoms, fintechs, and complex enterprises hard-wire cohesion into their ecosystems and realize measurable, strategic outcomes.

Mission

Make cohesive performance the default state for every enterprise ecosystem I touch.

Evidence-First Consulting

Every recommendation maps to a dependency model and a quantified value impact—never slideware guesswork.

Capability Transfer

I embed knowledge and coach internal teams so momentum continues long after project close.

Vendor-Neutral Integrity

Eidos resells no tooling; advice remains agenda-free and aligned solely to client outcomes.
