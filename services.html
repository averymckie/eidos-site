Services

What We Provide

Structural assessment before you commit capital.

The Situations

When People Hire Us

Before a major investment

You have approved a budget for automation and vendors are presenting solutions. Before you sign, you want to know if the underlying processes can actually support what is being proposed. The assessment tells you where the structural barriers are and what would need to change before automation could succeed.

After something has not worked

An automation initiative did not deliver what was expected, and before allocating more resources, you want to understand the structural reasons. The operational post-mortem tells you what went wrong. The structural analysis tells you why the failure was predictable and whether the next attempt will face the same barriers.

During evaluation of a company

You are assessing automation claims as part of due diligence, whether for an acquisition, investment, or partnership. You want an independent analysis of whether those claims are structurally realistic or depend on tacit knowledge that may not transfer.

Deliverables

What You Receive

Process documentation

A complete map of the process as it actually operates, including exception paths that may not appear in existing documentation. This often reveals that the process is more complex than stakeholders realize because processes evolve faster than their documentation does.

Automation profile

Each segment of the process assessed for automation potential. This shows where automation can work and where it cannot, with clear rationale for each determination.

Risk analysis

A clear assessment of the gap between what is being proposed and what the process structure can support. If vendors are promising outcomes the process cannot deliver, the analysis identifies this.

Recommendation

One of three answers: proceed because the structure supports it, restructure first before automating, or do not automate because the barriers are structural rather than technical. With the evidence behind the recommendation.
